About Us

State Life Insurance was chosen as the new name after a thorough research exercise. The word “Jubilee” is already well-known in the financial services market and is associated with “Happiness” which very much relates to our business. We therefore decided to give you the confidence of our strong presence in the market coupled with a promise to help you plan and realize your financial dreams to keep you and your loved ones happy at all times. With a global brand we will bring you global best practices in services and world class solutions to your financial needs.
